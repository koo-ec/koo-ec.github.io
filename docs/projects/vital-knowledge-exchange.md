# Vital Knowledge Exchange Visits

**Bolstering Ongoing Collaborative Responsible AI Projects at IIT-M and UoH**

This project supported knowledge exchange between the University of Hull and IIT Madras. The work strengthened responsible AI collaborations across shared interests such as trustworthy AI, human-centred deployment, digital health, smart-city sensing, and dependable machine-learning methods.

## Project Details

| Item | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Type | Knowledge exchange |
| Status | Complete |
| Funder | University of Hull |
| Value | £15,000 |
| Dates | 1 February 2024 - 31 July 2024 |
| Partner | Indian Institute of Technology Madras |
| Koorosh's role | Principal Investigator |

## Project Focus

- Building sustained collaboration between UoH and IIT Madras researchers.
- Strengthening responsible AI research links across health, smart cities, and dependable systems.
- Supporting visits, project planning, and future joint research activity.
- Translating responsible AI methods into cross-institutional research programmes.
