AI Safety & Multimodal Alignment

Bridging the gap between safe intelligence and real-world AI systems.

I am an Assistant Professor at the University of Hull working at the intersection of AI safety, multimodal alignment, and agent–human interaction. My research focuses on building AI systems that are robust, interpretable, and accountable in safety-critical settings. I lead the development of methods such as SafeML, SMILE / gSMILE, and TrustLLM to monitor, explain, and calibrate modern machine learning and foundation models.
