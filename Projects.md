## Key Research Projects & Open Source

**SafeML Framework** | [GitHub: 200+ stars](https://github.com/ISorokos/SafeML) (2020--Present)
*   Statistical-based safety monitoring for ML classifiers using PyTorch/TensorFlow
*   **Recommended in German Industry Standard DIN SPEC 92005** for ML uncertainty quantification
*   Extended for multimodal Vision-Language Models and time-series applications
*   Applied to autonomous systems, medical AI, and safety-critical applications

**TrustLLM - AI Alignment Research** | **$35K Grant PI** (2024--Present)
*   Developing **agent-human interaction workflows** for legal AI systems using JAX/Transformers
*   Research on **collaborative specification of alignment objectives** with modern prompting
*   Focus on **multimodal alignment** combining text and document understanding
*   Partnership with Walton&Co Ltd for real-world deployment in legal institutions

**XWhy/SMILE - Robust Explainability** | [GitHub](https://github.com/Dependable-Intelligent-Systems-Lab/xwhy) (2022--Present)
*   Statistical Model-agnostic Interpretability with Local Explanations for multimodal systems
*   Published in **IEEE Software** with focus on human-AI collaboration and trust
*   Applied to Vision-Language Models for explainable safety assessment

**SafeDrones - Real-time AI Safety** | [GitHub](https://github.com/koo-ec/SafeDrones) (2019--2022)
*   Real-time reliability evaluation framework for UAV swarms using executable digital twins
*   Developed **agentic coordination algorithms** for safe multi-drone operations
*   Published at International Symposium on Model-Based Safety and Assessment (Springer)

**SafeLLM - Domain-Specific LLM Safety** | Research Framework (2023--Present)
*   Domain-specific safety monitoring for Large Language Models in wind maintenance
*   Combines statistical methods with transformer architectures for safety assessment
*   Published in IOP Journal of Physics with practical deployment results
